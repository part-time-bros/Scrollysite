Different.

Six defining technologies that separate the M5 CS from everything else on the road.

01
Carbon Fibre Roof
Full-length CFRP panel. 40% lighter than steel. Lowers centre of gravity by 4mm for measurably sharper dynamics.
02
Titanium Exhaust
First production M car with titanium exhaust. 7.2 kg lighter than steel. Acoustically sharper, distinctly more intense.
03
M xDrive AWD
Rear-biased all-wheel drive with true 2WD mode. Torque vectoring diff distributes power with surgical precision.
04
Michelin Cup 2 R
Co-developed exclusively for the M5 CS. Semi-slick compound. Racetrack grip in road-legal form. Nothing held back.
05
Carbon Ceramic Brakes
Standard fitment. 6-piston front calipers. Fade-free performance across hundreds of consecutive hard stops.
06
Hand-Signed Engine
Each S63 unit assembled by one engineer who signs the crankcase. 6-hour bench test before installation. Non-negotiable.
